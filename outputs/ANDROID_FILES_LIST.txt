Files Required for Android Deployment
==================================================

Copy these files from outputs/ to app/src/main/assets/:

1. emergency_classifier.tflite (TFLite model)
2. vocab.txt (Vocabulary - includes [unused] tokens - this is normal!)
3. tokenizer_config.json (Tokenizer configuration)
4. special_tokens_map.json (Special tokens mapping)
5. label_mapping.json (Label to ID mapping)

Optional:
- deployment_info.json (Model metadata)
- ANDROID_DEPLOYMENT_GUIDE.md (Detailed integration guide)

==================================================
IMPORTANT: The '[unused]' tokens in vocab.txt are NORMAL!
They are part of DistilBERT vocabulary - DO NOT remove them.
